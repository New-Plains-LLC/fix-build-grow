# AI Optimization Strategy (GEO) for firsthomefix.com

## Overview
This document outlines the comprehensive AI optimization strategy for the firsthomefix.com website, designed to maximize content citation potential across AI platforms while adapting to the changing AI landscape.

## Core Principles

### 1. AI-First Content Design
- **Structure content for AI citation**, not just human readers
- **Include detailed technical specifications** that AI can reference accurately
- **Answer common DIY questions** explicitly with detailed responses
- **Format for easy extraction** by AI assistants

### 2. Multi-Platform Optimization
- Optimize content for different AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, etc.)
- Understand different citation styles and preferences across platforms
- Create content that performs well across AI ecosystems

### 3. Continuous Adaptation
- Monitor AI citation patterns and performance
- Update content based on how AI references and uses information
- Test against new AI models as they emerge
