## Domain Selection Criteria

### SEO Considerations:
- **Exact Match Potential:** Domains include key keywords
- **Search Volume:** Home improvement keywords have strong volume
- **Competition:** Less competitive than broad "DIY" terms
- **Local SEO:** Strong GEO targeting capabilities

### Brand Considerations:
- **Memorability:** All options are easy to remember
- **Trust:** Clear names build immediate trust
- **Expansion:** Options allow for future content expansion
- **Professionalism:** Appropriate for commercial use

### Technical Considerations:
- **Length:** All options are concise (12-17 characters)
- **Pronunciation:** Easy to say and spell
- **Availability:** All recommended domains are available
- **Extension:** .com preferred for maximum credibility
