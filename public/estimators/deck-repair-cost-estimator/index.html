Deck Repair Cost Estimator

A template for high-intent estimator content tied to materials, tools, and service referrals.

How to use this page

Break the project into structure, decking surface, fasteners, railing, and finish. That framing naturally supports links to boards, stains, hidden fasteners, and inspection checklists.

Estimator content blocks

  • Regional labor notes
  • Material quantity cheat sheet
  • Repair vs replace decision tree
Estimator assumptions
  • Assumes standard pressure-treated lumber sizes
  • Labor ranges vary by region and railing complexity
  • Permit costs are excluded unless structural framing changes
Budget ranges
  • Minor board swap: $300-$900
  • Surface + railing refresh: $1,200-$2,800
  • Partial structural rebuild: $2,500-$5,000+
